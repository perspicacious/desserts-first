Desserts First!

"Life is uncertain, eat desserts first!"

Welcome to Desserts First. Our lives are so full of activity that before we know it, the day is over. Many people share the necessity to “make time for fun”. At Desserts First, we say “Life is uncertain, eat desserts first!” We cater to anyone with a sweet tooth and we take special pleasure in serving those who aren’t easily satisfied. There is a sweet dish for everyone here! On the chance that that isn’t true, our baking expert will create something for you at request*.
